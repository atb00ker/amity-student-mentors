Student Mentor System

A simple interface for freshers to meet a senior with similar goals.

How does this work?

Students from second year onwards get their name on the website, freshers can contact the students based on their goals. Please contact the senior with a convincing case of why they should invest their time to guide you.

Student Mentor Project

The aim of this project is to help first year students find people in the college who have similar interests and dreams and have spend time with the idea. Other than degree you get a community from your college. This is aimed to help establish strong communities.
